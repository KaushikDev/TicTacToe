Select 'O'(Nought) or 'X'(Cross) as human player.

Quick Rules:

1. Game begins only by clicking 'Play' button and selecting either 'O' or 'X' .

3. Human plays first by clicking a cell of their choice. AI plays second followed by human and so on.

4. First player to complete a row or column or diagonal with selected mark(i.e. 'O' or 'X') wins the game.

5. If you reset the game(after the game finishes or in middle of the game), rule number 1 follows again.
